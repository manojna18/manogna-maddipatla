Namaste! I'm glad you found your way to my corner of the internet! I'm Manogna, a technical communicator based on Detroit, Michigan.

Like all great works of art, my work in design and visual storytelling is still evolving. It began nearly a decade ago in southern India with a simple habit: writing about the books I was reading. That habit grew into a four-year journalism career, where I learned to simplify complex ideas in finance and science and where I first fell in love with immersive, visual storytelling.

As curiosity pushed me further, I began experimenting with code to make stories interactive, blending narrative, data, and design. Over time, that exploration evolved into a deeper fascination with how people experience information, and that is what ultimately brought me to UX.

Today, I bring together writing, storytelling, data visualization, and design to create experiences that inform and engage.
